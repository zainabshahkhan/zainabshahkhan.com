About Me

Zainab Shah Khan works with a team of students like herself as a Project Lead and Research Assistant at Power & Research Lab, NUST-PNEC; she is also a part of various student societies on campus such as the Education and Community Service Society in which she is actively involved in raising funds for social causes and visiting orphanages and old age homes. She is also a senior member of Team Envision, a student funded venture of 20 mechanical and electrical engineers that fabriate and manufacture fuel efficient gaosiline and electrical vehicles to participate in the international competition of Shell Eco Marathon Asia (SEMA). She designs communications campaigns, posters and video content and is a freelancing web developer in her remaining time. She is very interested in the intersection of media, entertainment, and technology and is focused on building software that creates immersive experiences for viewers.
